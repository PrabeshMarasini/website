Introduction

Large Language Models (LLMs) are a subset of artificial intelligence designed to process and generate human-like text.

They are based on advanced deep learning architectures, particularly transformer models.

Applications

LLMs have a wide range of applications, including:

  • Chatbots and virtual assistants
  • Content generation
  • Translation services

Challenges

Despite their capabilities, LLMs face several challenges:

  1. Bias in training data
  2. High computational requirements
  3. Interpretability and transparency

Conclusion

LLMs are transforming the landscape of natural language processing, but ethical and technical challenges remain.
